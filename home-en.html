Understanding Anxiety Disorders

Learn about anxiety disorders, their symptoms, and treatment options. You're not alone in this journey.

What Are Anxiety Disorders?

Anxiety disorders are a group of mental health conditions characterized by significant feelings of anxiety and fear. These feelings are strong enough to interfere with daily activities and can be persistent and overwhelming.

While it's normal to feel anxious sometimes, people with anxiety disorders experience frequent and intense anxiety that can be debilitating.

Common Signs of Anxiety

Anxiety can manifest in several ways, including:

  • Excessive worry about everyday situations
  • Rapid heartbeat and breathing
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Sleep problems
  • Tension and irritability

Getting Help

If you're experiencing symptoms of anxiety, know that help is available. Professional treatment can provide you with the tools and support needed to effectively manage anxiety.
