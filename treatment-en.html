Treatment Options for Anxiety

Discover effective ways to manage and overcome anxiety with professional help and self-care strategies.

Explore Treatments

Professional Treatment

Psychotherapy

Common therapeutic approaches include:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Exposure Therapy
  • Mindfulness-Based Therapy
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
Learn more about therapy types

Medication

Common medications prescribed for anxiety:

  • SSRIs (Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors)
  • SNRIs (Serotonin-Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors)
  • Anti-anxiety medications
  • Beta-blockers

Always consult a healthcare provider before starting any medication.

Lifestyle Changes

Exercise and Physical Activity

  • Regular aerobic exercise
  • Yoga and stretching
  • Walking in nature
  • Swimming

Stress Management

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Meditation and mindfulness
  • Time management techniques

Self-Help Strategies

Daily Practices

  • Maintaining a regular sleep schedule
  • Healthy eating habits
  • Limiting caffeine and alcohol
  • Journaling thoughts and feelings
  • Setting realistic goals

Recommended Apps

  • Calm - Meditation and Sleep
  • Headspace - Mindfulness
  • Anxiety Coach
  • Breathwrk - Breathing Exercises
