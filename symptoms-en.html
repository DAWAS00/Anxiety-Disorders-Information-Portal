Recognizing Anxiety Symptoms

Understanding the signs and symptoms of anxiety is the first step toward getting help.

Physical Symptoms

  • Rapid heartbeat
  • Sweating
  • Trembling or shaking
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or tightness
  • Nausea or stomach problems
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Muscle tension

Emotional Symptoms

  • Excessive worry
  • Feeling of impending doom
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Feeling on edge
  • Difficulty controlling worry
  • Fear of losing control

Cognitive Symptoms

  • Racing thoughts
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Memory problems
  • Trouble making decisions
  • Mind going blank
  • Overthinking

Behavioral Symptoms

  • Avoiding situations
  • Procrastination
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Changes in eating habits
  • Social withdrawal
  • Seeking reassurance

When to Seek Help

Consider seeking professional help if your symptoms:

  • Interfere with daily activities
  • Affect your relationships
  • Impact your work or studies
  • Persist for extended periods
  • Cause significant distress
